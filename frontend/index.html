Welcome to the Statistics Online Computing Platform

The goals of this platform are developing a dynamic, interrative platform trying to help students to better understand statistical ideas and providing convenience for statistical teaching. By logging into this online platform, teachers can select the tools that need to be explained according to the actual situation of teaching, combine with the examples from the textbook, and assist teaching with vivid dynamic interactive statistical diagrams to help students deeply understand randomness and related important statistical ideas.

The general framework of this platform is based on the textbook 现代基础统计学, combined with the keypoints and examples in the book. The platform is developed using R package: Shiny.
